FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need to download an app to order?▾
No install needed to scan and order. The QR menu opens in your browser — works on any phone, any browser, instantly. The Avenzo iOS and Android app is coming soon and will unlock a whole lot more for frequent diners.
Is Avenzo only for Bengaluru?▾
We're born in Bengaluru but definitely not limited to it. We're expanding across all cities and states in India. If you're a restaurant owner anywhere in the country, reach out — we'd love to have you on board.
Which restaurant types does Avenzo support?▾
Avenzo supports dine-in restaurants, self-service counters, cafés, food courts and more. The platform is highly customisable — restaurants can configure it to match exactly how they operate.
Can the whole table order together?▾
Yes — everyone at the table can scan the same QR and order from their own phones. All orders for a table are grouped together so the kitchen gets one clear view.
How do I pay?▾
UPI, debit card, credit card — all supported securely. You can also pay cash at the table if the restaurant allows it.
I run a restaurant. How do I join?▾
Sign up on our partner page, build your menu with photos, configure your restaurant type, and we generate QR codes for every table or counter. Most restaurants are live within the hour.
