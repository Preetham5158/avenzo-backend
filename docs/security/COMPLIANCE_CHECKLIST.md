# Compliance Checklist

Template only. Needs legal review before production use.

- DPDP awareness for Indian users.
- Clear consent/notice for phone, account, order, lead, and notification data.
- Privacy policy published and linked.
- Terms and refund policy published and reviewed.
- Deletion/correction request process.
- Data retention policy for orders, leads, logs, and notifications.
- Breach response owner and procedure.
- Children/minors caution in public flows.
- Payment compliance review before payment launch.
- SMS/email/WhatsApp consent and transactional notification wording.
- Avoid false legal compliance claims until reviewed.

## Archive Safety

- Never share a zip containing `.env`, `.git`, `node_modules`, screenshots, temp files, or production exports.
- Rotate secrets if `.env` was shared.
- Keep local development DB and live DB separate.
