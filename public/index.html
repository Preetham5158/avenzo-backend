How it works
Step 01 — Arrive

Scan the QR
at your table

No app to install. No account to create. Open your camera, point it at the QR on the table, and the full menu opens instantly in your browser.

Step 02 — Browse

Take your time.
Order when ready.

Browse every dish, see prices, add to your cart. No waiter hovering. No pressure. You're in control of your own pace.

Step 03 — Order

Kitchen gets it
the instant you confirm

Tap confirm and your order lands on the kitchen display in milliseconds — exact, clear, and in sequence. Zero noise, zero errors.

Step 04 — Enjoy

Food arrives.
Everyone's happy.

Track your order live. Get notified the moment it's ready. No mystery, no stress — just the experience dining should always feel like.
