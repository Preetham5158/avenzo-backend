FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need to download an app?▾
No app, ever. Scan the QR code with your phone camera and the menu opens in your browser instantly. Works on iPhone and Android — any phone, any browser.
Which restaurants are on Avenzo?▾
We're live with restaurants across Bengaluru — from neighbourhood tiffin spots to multi-cuisine lounges. Browse the full list on our restaurants page.
Can the whole table order together?▾
Yes — everyone at the table can scan their own QR and order from their phone. All orders for the same table are grouped together so the kitchen gets a clear picture.
How do I pay?▾
UPI, debit card, credit card — all supported through Razorpay's secure gateway. You can also choose to pay cash at the table if the restaurant allows it.
I run a restaurant. How do I join?▾
Sign up on our partner page, build your menu with photos and prices, and we'll generate QR codes for every table. Most restaurants are live within an hour.
