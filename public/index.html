How it works
Step 01 — Arrive

Scan the QR at
your table

No app to download. No account to create. Open your phone camera, point it at the QR code on the table, and your menu opens instantly.

Step 02 — Browse

Browse at your
own pace

See every dish, price, and photo. No waiter hovering. No pressure. Take your time — add to cart when you're ready, and order when you want.

Your order goes straight to the kitchen display — crystal clear, no noise, no errors. The chef sees it the moment you tap confirm.
